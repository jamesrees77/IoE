What did we do

We have created a glove that will help communicate between people who can and can not use sign language. We have created a glove, that when connected to our app will repeat back the letters of the sign language alphebet based on the users hand movement.

02

Why did we do it?

We created this as a way of making university life more accessible for students with hearing difficulties. This device will allow people to communicate through sign language, even to those who do not know it.

Larger scale issues

We also identified the problem that there are many different sign languages all around the world. Our device will help communicate these differences.
