We've created a sign language glove!

Arduino

We used an Arduino Uno to create our device that can be used to show sign language on our app.

Sign Language

We created our device to allow easier communication between people who can and can not speak sign language

IoT

Our device links to this web-app (in the app page). Wearing the glove and performing the sign language alphabet will display it in our app.
